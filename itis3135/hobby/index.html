Why

Cooking can be done for necessity for survival. As as humans or any species has to get fuel somehow. For some organisms, plants, sunglight is enough for fuel and that is their food. Humans eat for survival but that is not the only reason we cook. Cooking is done for cultural reasons, religion and every aspect of our lives. Cooking brings people together and gives them a sense of belonging and nostalgia. Cooking can also be a hobby. People cook as a hobby they gather recipes or curate them on their own. Cooking requires science and quite a bit of chemistry. People research and find ways to cook food properly for the most optimal falvour profile.

Cooking is a culture and that is why it is also a hobby. Figuring out what people eat and why they do is also anthropology on its own. Cooking is a form of art to many from curating recipes to preparing plates. That is why people cook as a hobby. It is art, history and scinece. These are all hobbies that can be translated as a form of cooking.
